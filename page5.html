Ice Cream Brands

Edy's the ice cream brand. If you like the classics get slow churned, and you will like the new flavors just turn into plain regular, either way you're left satisfied at the end. Slow-churned has no artificial flavors and just a simple recipe. The regular one may have artificial flavors but it does have one thing for sure yummy amazing flavors.
